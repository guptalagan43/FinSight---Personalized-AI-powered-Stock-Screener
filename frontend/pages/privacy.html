1. Introduction

FinSight Analytics ("FinSight", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users ("you", "your"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit and use our platform.

By using FinSight, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, gender, occupation, and other profile details you voluntarily provide during registration or profile updates.
  • Investment Preferences: Risk profile, experience level, income range, investment goals, and similar preferences you configure.
  • Watchlists & Portfolio: The instruments you add to your watchlists and portfolio tracking features.
  • Communications: Any messages, feedback, or correspondence you send to us.

Information collected automatically:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, search queries, and interaction patterns.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and device identifiers.
  • Log Data: IP address, access timestamps, referring URLs, and error logs.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create, maintain, and secure your account
  • Personalize your experience and provide relevant content
  • Operate and improve our platform's features and services
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Generate anonymized, aggregated analytics to improve the platform
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to any third parties. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party services that help us operate the platform (e.g., hosting, analytics) under strict data protection agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • Safety: To protect the rights, property, or safety of FinSight, our users, or the public.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user data may be transferred as part of the transaction.

5. Data Storage & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS/SSL
  • Passwords stored using one-way cryptographic hashing (bcrypt)
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security reviews and updates

While we strive to protect your data, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Cookies & Local Storage

FinSight uses browser local storage (not traditional cookies) to:

  • Store your authentication token for session management
  • Remember your theme preference (light/dark mode)
  • Cache user profile data for improved performance

You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings. Clearing local storage will log you out and reset your preferences.

7. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you

  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data

  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@finsight.ai.

Community Guidelines

Building a respectful, informed, and safe community for all investors

FinSight is built for the community of retail investors who seek knowledge, transparency, and better decision-making tools. These guidelines define how we expect every user to engage with the platform and each other.

🤝

Be Respectful

Treat every user with dignity and respect. Offensive language, harassment, bullying, hate speech, and discriminatory behaviour are strictly prohibited.

Share Responsibly

Do not provide unsolicited investment advice or recommendations. All discussions should be educational and analytical. Never present opinions as financial advice.

🚫

No Market Manipulation

Any attempt to manipulate markets, spread false information about stocks, or coordinate artificial price movements is strictly forbidden and may be reported to authorities.

Protect Your Account

Never share your login credentials, OTPs, or personal information with other users. FinSight will never ask for your password via email, chat, or phone.

⚖️

Comply with Laws

All users must comply with applicable laws and regulations including SEBI guidelines and the Information Technology Act, 2000. Engaging in insider trading or any form of market fraud is illegal.

🧹

No Spam or Solicitation

Do not use FinSight to distribute spam, advertise third-party products, promote competing services, or solicit investments. Such activities will result in immediate account suspension.

Enforcement

Violation of these guidelines may result in the following actions at FinSight's discretion:

  • Warning and content removal
  • Temporary suspension of account access
  • Permanent ban from the platform
  • Reporting to relevant authorities in case of legal violations

If you believe a user is violating these guidelines, please report the issue to trust@finsight.ai.

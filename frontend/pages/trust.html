Trust & Safety

Your security and trust are our highest priority

Secure Authentication

Passwords are hashed using bcrypt. We support OTP-based verification for added account security.

FinSight does not process payments or execute trades. Your financial information is never at risk on our platform.

👁️

Privacy First

We collect only the minimum data needed to operate. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

How We Keep You Safe

At FinSight, security isn't an afterthought — it's built into the core of our platform. Here's how:

  • Secure Sessions: Authentication tokens are time-limited and securely stored. Inactive sessions are automatically terminated.
  • Email & OTP Verification: Account creation and password resets require multi-step verification to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Role-Based Access: Administrative functions are restricted to verified admin users only. Regular users cannot access or modify system-level data.
  • Input Validation: All user inputs are sanitized and validated on both frontend and backend to prevent injection attacks.
  • Regular Security Audits: We periodically review our codebase and infrastructure for vulnerabilities.

Responsible Disclosure

If you discover a security vulnerability in FinSight, we appreciate your help in disclosing it to us responsibly. Please email security@finsight.ai with details of the vulnerability. We commit to:

  • Acknowledging your report within 48 hours
  • Investigating the issue promptly
  • Keeping you informed about the resolution
  • Giving credit (with your consent) for valid findings

Reporting Abuse

If you encounter any content, behaviour, or user activity on FinSight that you believe violates our Community Guidelines or Terms of Use, please report it to trust@finsight.ai. We take all reports seriously and will investigate promptly.
